About Us

Barunastra Team was formed from the November 10 Institute of Technology, a robot team focused on competition for the development of unmanned transportation, namely autonomous ships. The autonomous ship developed by the Barunastra Team was created for various purposes in accordance with the theme being contested with the current urgency, such as ocean exploration and the defense and security of the sea area in a country.

Barunastra comes from two words namely "Varuna" and "Astra" which means the weapon of Baruna god or the god of the sea in Hindu mythology. Baruna's weapon has a shape in the form of a net that makes our team have hopes to become a strong network in ITS capable of mastering marine technology.
